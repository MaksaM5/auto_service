About us

Auto Service Servis, established in 2001, has become a trusted name in the automotive service industry. Known for our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction, we have built a reputation for providing top-quality repairs and maintenance services. At Auto Service Servis, we pride ourselves on our skilled technicians who are dedicated to keeping vehicles running smoothly and safely. Whether it's routine maintenance or complex repairs, we strive to deliver reliable service that exceeds our customers' expectations. Our mission is to ensure every vehicle leaves our service center in optimal condition, backed by years of expertise and a passion for automotive care.


Employees



Mechanic

Mechanics diagnose and repair vehicle issues, ensuring cars are safe and running efficiently.


Service Advisor

Service advisors liaise between customers and the service department, providing guidance on vehicle maintenance and repair options.


Automotive Technician

Automotive technicians perform skilled tasks such as engine diagnostics, brake repairs, and electrical system troubleshooting.


Parts Specialist

Parts specialists manage inventory, source and order parts, and ensure the availability of necessary components for repairs.


Detailer

Detailers clean and prepare vehicles, ensuring they are spotless inside and out before returning them to customers.


Customer Service Representative

Customer service representatives handle inquiries, appointments, and feedback, ensuring a smooth and pleasant experience for clients visiting the auto service.
