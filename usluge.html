Our Services

Regular Vehicle Maintenance

  • Oil and Filter Replacement: Regular replacement of engine oil, oil filter, air filter, and fuel filter.

  • Brake Inspection and Adjustment: Checking brakes, replacing brake pads, brake discs, and brake fluid.

  • Fluid Check and Refill: Adding antifreeze, windshield washer fluid, and other essential fluids.

Engine Repairs and Servicing

  • Engine Diagnostics and Repairs: Checking engine performance, identifying and fixing issues.

  • Engine Parts Replacement: Timing belt, chain, spark plugs, fuel and air filters.

  • Engine Tuning: Adjusting the engine's operational settings, optimizing performance.

Brake Repairs and Replacements

  • Brake Pad Replacement: Removing and replacing worn-out brake pads.

  • Brake Disc or Drum Replacement: Replacing damaged or worn brake discs or drums.

  • Brake Hose Inspection and Replacement: Replacing hoses if they are damaged or worn.

  • Brake Adjustment: Adjusting brakes to ensure proper functioning.

  • Brake Fluid Check and Replacement: Replacing brake fluid if contaminated or worn.

Air Conditioning Servicing and Repair

  • Air Conditioning Check and Recharge: Checking the air conditioning system and recharging the refrigerant.

  • Component Repair and Replacement: Compressor, condenser, evaporator, and other air conditioning system parts.

Tire Repairs and Replacements

  • Tire Balancing and Rotation: Balancing wheels and rotating tires to extend their lifespan.

  • Tire Replacement and Repair: Replacing worn-out or damaged tires, repairing punctured tires.

Electrical and Electronic System

  • Electrical Problem Diagnostics: Identifying and fixing issues with electrical systems, such as batteries, alternators, and starters.

  • Electrical Component Repair and Replacement: Bulbs, fuses, switches, and other electrical parts.

Suspension System Servicing and Repair

  • Shock Absorber and Spring Repair or Replacement: Checking and replacing shock absorbers and springs to improve ride quality and stability.

  • Steering System Inspection and Repair: Repairing or replacing steering system components, such as the steering wheel and mechanism.

Preparation for Technical Inspection

  • Vehicle Preparation for Technical Inspection: Ensuring the vehicle meets all technical requirements and performing necessary repairs before the inspection.

Vehicle Washing and Detailing

  • Exterior and Interior Cleaning: Washing the vehicle's exterior, detailed cleaning of the interior, including vacuuming, cleaning carpets, and seats.

Glass Replacement and Repair

  • Windshield Repair or Replacement: Repairing minor damage or replacing the entire windshield if severely damaged.

  • Side and Rear Glass Repair or Replacement: Replacing broken or damaged side and rear windows.
